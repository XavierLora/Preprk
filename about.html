Welcome to Preprk!

We are a company that helps people reserve and sell parking spots for events. Our platform makes it easy to find a spot or list one for resale, so you can arrive at sporting events, concerts, and other major mass traffic events with peace of mind.

Preprk was founded by a team of creative problem solvers who saw an opportunity to improve the parking experience for event attendees. We are dedicated to providing a seamless and hassle-free service, and we are constantly striving to innovate and improve.

Thank you for choosing Preprk! We hope you have a great experience using our platform. If you have any questions or feedback, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Q: How does Preprk work?

A: Preprk is a platform that allows you to reserve parking spots for events. If you are attending an event and need a parking spot, you can search for available spots on our website and reserve one that meets your needs.

Q: How do I search for a parking spot?

A: To search for a parking spot, simply visit our website and use the search form to input the details of the event you are attending (event name, location, date). You will then be able to view a map or list of available parking spots in the area, and filter the results by price, distance, and type of spot (e.g. covered, reserved, etc.). When you find a spot that you like, you can reserve it through our secure checkout process.

Q: How do I pay for a parking spot?

A: When you reserve a parking spot through Preprk, you will be asked to provide payment information through our secure checkout process. We accept major credit cards and debit cards.

Q: Can I cancel a reservation or a listing?

A: Yes, you can cancel a reservation through your Preprk account. Simply log in to your account and navigate to the "My Reservations" section. From there, you should be able to cancel the reservation or listing by following the prompts. Please note that cancellations may be subject to fees or penalties, depending on the terms and conditions of your reservation.

Q: What happens if the event is cancelled or rescheduled?

A: If the event you have reserved a parking spot for is cancelled or rescheduled, you will have the option to request a refund or transfer the reservation to the new event date. Please note that these options may be subject to fees or penalties, depending on the terms and conditions of your reservation or listing.
